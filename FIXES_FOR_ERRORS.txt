CHECKOUT TIME RULES DETAILS
===============================================================================

CHECK-IN RULES (Already implemented):
- Before 9:05 AM → Present (1.0)
- 9:05 AM - 9:30 AM → Late within buffer (1.0)
- After 9:30 AM → Half day (0.5)

CHECKOUT RULES (Now implemented):
- Before 5:30 PM (17:30) → Half day deduction (-0.5)
- After 5:30 PM → No penalty

COMBINED SCENARIOS:
1. Check-in: 9:00 AM (1.0) + Checkout: 6:00 PM (no penalty) = 1.0 (Full day)
2. Check-in: 9:00 AM (1.0) + Checkout: 4:00 PM (-0.5) = 0.5 (Half day)
3. Check-in: 10:00 AM (0.5) + Checkout: 6:00 PM (no penalty) = 0.5 (Half day)
4. Check-in: 10:00 AM (0.5) + Checkout: 4:00 PM (-0.5) = 0.0 (Absent)
