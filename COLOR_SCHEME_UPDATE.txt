BLUE COLOR SCHEME:
✓ Professional and corporate

✓ Conveys stability and reliability

✓ Modern and clean aesthetic
✓ Gender-neutral and universally appealing

AVOIDED:
❌ Purple - Too playful/creative for business
❌ Green - Often reserved for success/completion states
❌ Red - Associated with errors/warnings

BLUE IS PERFECT FOR:
✓ Task management tools
✓ Project management
✓ Team collaboration
✓ Business applications
✓ Professional environments
