PREDEFINED LABELS:
------------------
The following labels are available by default:
- PRIORITY (red)
- IN PROCESS (orange)
- TASK COMPLETE (green)
- TASK DELAYED (orange)
- ON HOLD (gray)
- HELP NEEDED (purple)
- COPY REVIEW NEEDED (blue)
- WAITING ON SIGN OFF (cyan)

HOW LABELS ARE STORED:
-----------------------
Labels are stored as a JSONB array in the tasks table:
Example: [{"id": "priority"}, {"id": "in-process"}]
